February 2025
BBMP Mindmasters – 2025
Description/Highlights/website/social media/pictures
The BBMP Mindmasters – 2025 was organized in association with the BBMP Employees Welfare Development Association. Hosted on the February 16, 2025, at the BBMP Head Office Premises, the event featured a total prize fund of ₹60,000/-, and offered a competitive platform for both open and age-category players.
The tournament followed a rapid format (20 minutes + 5 seconds increment per round) and consists of multiple rounds throughout the day, concluding with a prize distribution ceremony. Special prizes were awarded to the best veteran, best woman player, best differently-abled player, and youngest participants.
 
April 2025
2nd Namma Bengaluru International Grandmasters Open Chess Tournament – 2025
Description/Highlights/website/social media/pictures
The 2nd Namma Bengaluru International Grandmasters Open Chess Tournament – 2025 is set to be one of India's premier chess events, bringing together over 2000 players from across the globe to compete at the Koramangala Indoor Stadium, Bengaluru, from 10th to 18th April 2025. This tournament will feature elite Grandmasters, International Masters, and rising chess talents, making it a landmark event in the Indian chess calendar.
With global recognition, extensive media coverage, and strong support from the Government of Karnataka, the tournament aims to solidify Bengaluru’s position as a hub for world-class chess.
2nd Namma Bengaluru International Open FIDE Rated Blitz Chess Tournament - 2025
The Namma Bengaluru International GM Open – Blitz Tournament 2025 is an Open FIDE Rated Blitz Chess Tournament set to take place on April 14, 2025, at Koramangala Indoor Stadium. Organized as part of the prestigious Namma Bengaluru International Grandmasters Tournament, this event offers a fast-paced, high-intensity action.
With a total prize fund of ₹4,00,000, the tournament features multiple prize categories. The event welcomes chess enthusiasts of all levels, with free entry for GMs, IMs, WGMs, and WIMs. Open to Indian and international players.
