Our Plan-of-action: (to be done like a flow-chat or using infographics)
We have structured our plan of action around three core focus areas.
Socio-chess activities: We perceive Chess as a tool for social connection. Through innovative formats, outreach events, and interactive sessions, we aim to break barriers and bring chess to a wider audience.
Bengaluru: A global chess hub: Our vision is to transform Bengaluru into a world-class centre for chess. By organizing elite tournaments, strengthening grassroots programs, and building strategic alliances, we are paving the way for the city to become a key player on the global chess map.
Women in Chess: Our initiatives are designed to inspire women to pursue chess professionally and emerge as new champions in the field. We are dedicated to empowering female talent by providing specialized mentorship, and competitive opportunities.

Bangalore International Grandmasters Tournament Organizing Committee:
In line with the mission of the Golden Aura Trust to promote chess and the spirit of excellence, an exclusive committee, the Bangalore International Grandmasters Tournament Organizing Committee is established. This committee handles the planning and execution of the 2nd Namma Bengaluru International Grandmasters Tournament in, scheduled for April 2025.
